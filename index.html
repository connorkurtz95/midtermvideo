Interests

In this section, one should include their interests outside of school and work. People may think that this is a waste of time, or irrelevent in a professional setting, but including your interests helps give you a personality, and helps companies understand more about the person who they might hire.

Personally, I love playing and writing music, and watching films. Make sure your interests don't make it sound like you won't have time to work!
